About AQRAM Studio

AQRAM Studio is a leading photography studio in Indonesia, known for its elegant and sophisticated approach to capturing moments. Our expertise ranges from event photography to commercial shoots, with a strong emphasis on creativity, precision, and customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality visual storytelling that resonates with our clients.

With a team of passionate photographers, designers, and creative professionals, AQRAM Studio is committed to pushing the boundaries of photography. Our unique perspective and innovative approach make us stand out in the industry. We believe in creating timeless memories that speak volumes through every image we capture.

We operate across multiple locations in Indonesia, ensuring that our clients have access to our services no matter where they are. From our headquarters in Jakarta to branches in Bandung and Surabaya, we strive to provide the best photography experiences with a personalized touch.

Our Headquarters

Jakarta (Main Office)

Our flagship office located in Jakarta is the heart of AQRAM Studio. As the primary headquarters, we handle key operations, project management, and serve as the main hub for our creative services and collaborations. Located at the vibrant center of Indonesia's capital, it is easily accessible for clients and partners.

Bandung

Bandung, known for its cool climate and vibrant culture, is home to our creative studio. This location offers an ideal setting for innovation, with a growing creative industry. We focus on offering a relaxed atmosphere for brainstorming, design, and photography work. It’s a perfect blend of modernity and natural beauty, attracting both local and international talent.

Surabaya

Surabaya serves as our expansion office, playing a key role in extending our network across Indonesia. This location emphasizes growing our presence in the business and creative sectors. With the strategic positioning as a major port city, Surabaya provides access to a wide range of opportunities, including cross-industry collaborations and access to new markets.
